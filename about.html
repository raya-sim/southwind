What we do

Breeding

Southwind horses and ponies are selectively bred from strong Foundation lines for athleticism, temperament, and suitability for the hunter and jumper rings.

Training

Individualized training programs are crafted to suit horses and riders, with a focus on building strong fundamentals.

Competition

From short stirrup to the grand prix, Southwind horses and riders excel in both national and international competition.

Sales

We frequently offer horses from our program for sale, and prioritize ensuring a perfect match between horse and rider.

Located in beautiful Ocala, Florida, Southwind is a lush haven for horse and rider alike.

The world-class World Equestrian Center is just a short drive away. But surrounded by old oaks dripping with Spanish moss, Southwind feels like a quiet escape.

Horses split their time between ample grassy turnout and roomy stalls in a newly-renovated state-of-the-art barn. In addition to a massive all-weather arena for schooling, trails run through the property and a grassy field is reserved for hacking out. And a beautiful tack room and comortable indoor-outdoor lounge area make the barn a place you'll never want to leave.

The Team

Oliver Berkshire

Owner

Oliver grew up riding as a child and teenager, but took several decades off after going to college and working in finance in New York and then Miami. In the late-2010s, a renewed interest in riding soon escalated into buying land for a boarding farm and starting the next chapter. Now free of the office, Oliver puts his business experience to work keeping the farm running and the horses happy.

Raya Carlisle

Head Trainer

Raya has been around horses for 30 years and competitive for nearly as many. She spent her teens and twenties training with and working for the top jumper riders in the U.S. and Europe and quickly became known for her precision and focus both in and out of the arena. At Southwind, Raya heads up all aspects of training and horse management.
