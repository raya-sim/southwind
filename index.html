Philosophy

Excellence isn’t born in the show ring – it starts at home. At Southwind, we believe in building a foundation for success through careful breeding, a serene environment, and an emphasis on the fundamentals. All of our horses – from the grand prix stallion to the short stirrup pony – are treated like both athletes and members of the family.

Each horse is placed in an individualized program designed to keep them healthy, happy, and competitive. Just as important, our students are not only strong and precise in the saddle, but excellent horsemen who learn to put the horse first and let success follow.

Raya Carlisle - Head Trainer - Southwind
