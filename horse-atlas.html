Responsive • Curious • Insecure

Atlas is one of those horses who truly wants to be a partner. He can be a bit insecure and cautious with new riders and handlers, but once he's developed a relationship, he's eager to listen and work together. You don't have to ask - you think and he responds. That connection and his inherent athleticism make him unstoppable with a good rider.
